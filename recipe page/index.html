Chicken Biryani Receipe

Chicken Biryani is a delicious and aromatic asian dish that combines flavorful rice with tender chicken and a blend of spices.

Ingredients

For Marinating Chicken

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste

For rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste

For biryani masala

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, mix together yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, coat them well, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
